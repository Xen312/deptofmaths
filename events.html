Events and Works

A list of all the events organized by the Students' Forum for Mathematics, DMU along with the Department of Mathematics, DMU. Press on the event to see pictures from that event

27/10/2025

A new Convenor, a Treasurer, a General Auditor and three Co-convenors of the DM College of Science, DM College of Arts and GP Women's College along with executive members from the four Semesters of UG were selected in a meeting attended by the previous members of the Students' Forum for Mathematics, DMU for the term 2025-2026.

15/02/2025

The prize distribution ceremony for the 1st State Level Mathematics Mock Test, organized by the Students' Forum for Mathematics, DMU was held at the Centrall Hall of DM College of Science.

12/01/2025

The 1st State Level Mathematics Mock Test, organized by the Students' Forum for Mathematics, DMU for HSLCE appearing students was held at DM College of Arts.

22/12/2024

Second day of the 2 days National Mathematics Day celebration. The second function was held at the Central Hall of DM College of Science.

21/12/2024

First day of the 2 days National Mathematics Day celebration. The first function was held at the Central Hall of GP Women's College.

05/11/2024

A meeting was held at GP Women's College to discuss about the celebration of the upcoming National Mathematics on the 22nd December of 2024. Preparation of a State Level Mock Test for the HSLC Examination appearing students were also discussed.

29/10/2024

A new Convenor, three Co-convenors of the DM College of Science, DM College of Arts and GP Women's College along with executive members from the three Semesters of UG were selected in a meeting attended by the previous members of the Students' Forum for Mathematics, DMU for the term 2024-2025.

28th August, 2024

The forum organized a one-day social service program on the 25th August, 2024.

13th May, 2024

A new Convenor-in-Charge was selected on 13th May, 2024. Two new Co-Convenor-in-Charges were also selected for DM Science and DM Arts.

6th May, 2024

The forum organized a fund drive to help the students affected by the hailstorm that tore down thousands of houses on 5th May, 2024.

29th Feb, 2024

After more than 4 months of construction, the forum, along with the Department of Mathematics, DM Science, inaugurated the Study Park of the Dept. of Mathematics, DM Science.

8th Jan, 2024

A general meeting was held at the central hall of DM College of Science for a significant expansion of the members of the forum, which was the inclusion of the students of DM Arts and GP Women's College.

22nd Dec, 2023

Organized by the forum, for the first time ever in DMU, National Mathematics Day was celebrated on the 22nd December 2023 at the Central Hall of DM Science.

14th Oct, 2023

The forum, along with the Department of Mathematics, DM Science, organized a one day social service cum tree plantation program.

9th Oct, 2023

The forum lodged a complaint to the VC of DMU for the scarcity of teachers in the Mathematics Department of DM Science.

26th Sept, 2023

A General Body meeting was held at the PG Block, Dept. of Mathematics, DMU in the presence of 250 students of Mathematics Dept. of DM Science and the Students' Forum for Mathematics was formed.
